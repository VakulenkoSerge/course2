Metalworking

We know how to provide high quality solutions

ETM offers a specific consultancy service to the Customer in the prototyping phase, in the feasibility analysis and in the evaluation of the material to be used. Our vast and long-standing experience covers the widest range of materials: carbon steels, low alloys, high alloys, gray cast iron, nodular cast iron up to stainless steels (including duplex and super duplex) and nickel-based alloys.

Our production sites meet the criteria of ISO 14001 (Environmental Management System) and BS OHSAS 18001 (Health and Safety Management System).
